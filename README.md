Tata Group Data Visualization Virtual Experience

Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Tata Group Virtual Internship via Forage. I acted as a Data Analyst to provide strategic business insights for an online retail store's CEO and CMO. The goal was to analyze 2011 sales data and provide recommendations for expansion and marketing for the upcoming year.

Data Cleaning & Preparation

Before visualization, I performed rigorous data cleaning using Power Query to ensure the integrity of the insights:
Removed Returns: Filtered out transactions with negative quantities.
Error Correction: Removed records with unit prices below zero.
Data Consistency: Ensured date formats and country names were standardized for accurate mapping.

Key Business Insights (The 4 Questions)

1. Revenue Trend & Seasonality Analysis (CEO)
Finding: Revenue remains steady for the first 8 months but shows a massive surge starting in September, peaking significantly in November.
Strategy: Recommended increasing inventory and marketing spend by October to capitalize on the year-end holiday demand.

3. Global Market Leaders (CMO)
Finding: Excluding the UK, the top revenue-generating countries are the Netherlands, Germany, and France.
Insight: These regions show high transaction volumes, indicating a strong existing brand presence and market fit.

5. Customer Concentration (CMO)
Finding: A small group of Top 10 High-Value Customers contributes a disproportionate amount of total revenue.
Strategy: Suggested a VIP loyalty program and personalized retention strategies to secure these core revenue streams.

7. Expansion Opportunities (CEO)
Finding: Using bubble map analysis, I identified high organic demand in regions like Australia, despite no dedicated marketing efforts there.
Recommendation: Australia and key European hubs are "Low-Risk, High-Reward" targets for physical expansion in 2012.
